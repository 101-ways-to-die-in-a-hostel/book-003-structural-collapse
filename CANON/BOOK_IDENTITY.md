## The Death (One Sentence)
A gap year student on a budget chooses the cheapest bed in an ancient colonial building, only to be crushed when centuries of deferred maintenance finally catch up with the structure during a tropical storm.

## Tagline
"She wanted an authentic experience. The building delivered - authentically lethal."

## Themes
1. The tension between "authentic" travel and safety
2. Colonial architecture as literal and metaphorical weight
3. The invisible cost of budget travel - someone always pays

## Target Reader
Gap year travelers who've slept in questionable buildings, anyone who's chosen atmosphere over safety, and travelers who've noticed cracks in walls and convinced themselves it's "character."
